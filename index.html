How to play

  1. Allow access to your microphone
  2. Turn on your speakers
  3. Move your hand past your microphone to make the bird fly upwards.

Tip: Trying different volume levels may make the game more responsive and give better results.

Device Support

Dopply Bird will only run on Google Chrome (Desktop or Android) dues to limitations in other browsers.

If you have trouble with the controls please visit http://danielrapp.github.io/doppler/ and try the demos which will give a better indication of how well this game will work for you.

Tested Devices

MacBook Air

Microphone is located on left side of device. Moving your hand from left to right over it works well. As does a down to up motion if the MacBook is on a raised platform.

Nexus 5

Support is a bit flakey. Better results can be achieved by tapping the microphone instead.
